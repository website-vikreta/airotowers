Need of Air Purification System

  • India has the most polluted cities in the world in 2018 (Source- IQ Air Visual World Air Quality Report).
  • Worldwide exposure to PM2.5 (Air Pollution) contributed to 4.1 million deaths from heart disease and stroke, lung cancer, chronic lung disease, and respiratory infections in 2016.PM 2.5 is very harmful because of its size.

Air Purfying units has various kinds of filters. These filters can remove particles, allergens, harmful gases, bacteria and viruses also particle matter like pollen, pet dander, and mold spores. Good air quality protects us from allergies, asthma,lung cancer, strokes, heart diseases and lung disease so we can all breathe a little easier.
